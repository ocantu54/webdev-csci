Overview

This website goes over the different variations and models of the new for 2024 Chevrolet Corvette and provides details about what separates them.

The Chevrolet Corvette Stingray

LT1:

The Chevrolet Corvette Stingray LT1 is the base trim of the mid-engine Corvette lineup, known for delivering supercar performance at a relatively affordable price. It features a naturally aspirated 6.2-liter V8 engine producing 490 horsepower (495 hp with the optional performance exhaust) and 465 lb-ft of torque, paired with an 8-speed dual-clutch automatic transmission. This combination allows the LT1 to go from 0-60 mph in around 2.9 seconds.

The LT1 trim offers a driver-focused interior with a digital gauge cluster, premium materials, and an 8-inch infotainment system with Apple CarPlay and Android Auto. It includes performance options like the Z51 Performance Package, which adds upgraded brakes, a performance exhaust, a rear spoiler, and an electronic limited-slip differential, enhancing the Stingray’s handling and braking.

With its aggressive design, balanced handling, and impressive performance, the LT1 stands out as an accessible entry point into the world of high-performance sports cars.

LT2:

The Chevrolet Corvette Stingray LT2 trim builds on the base LT1 by adding a suite of upgraded interior features and comfort enhancements. Like the LT1, it features a 6.2-liter V8 engine producing 490 horsepower (495 hp with the optional performance exhaust) and 465 lb-ft of torque, paired with an 8-speed dual-clutch transmission for swift shifts and a 0-60 mph time of about 2.9 seconds when equipped with the Z51 Performance Package.

In terms of features, the LT2 offers added convenience and luxury elements, including a premium 14-speaker Bose sound system, a heated steering wheel, wireless charging, and heated and ventilated seats. It also includes an upgraded interior trim with genuine leather accents, adding to the cabin's premium feel.

For drivers who prioritize added luxury alongside high performance, the LT2 strikes a balance, providing all the speed and handling of the LT1 but with extra comfort and tech features for a more refined driving experience.

LT3:

The Chevrolet Corvette Stingray LT3 is the top-tier trim of the Stingray lineup, offering the ultimate package of luxury, technology, and performance features. Featuring the same engine as the LT1 and LT2, this car produces the same 490 horsepower with the same 6.2-liter V8 engine.

The LT3 includes all the luxury amenities of the LT2, along with even more premium upgrades. This trim features GT2 sport seats as standard, which are crafted with Nappa leather and carbon-fiber trim for enhanced comfort and style. Additional perks include a custom leather-wrapped interior, a head-up display, and a Performance Data Recorder, allowing drivers to capture and analyze driving metrics. It also offers a surround-view camera system and a rear camera mirror, enhancing visibility and safety.

The LT3 is ideal for drivers looking for a fully loaded Corvette with high-end materials, advanced technology, and all the performance capabilities expected from the Stingray. It represents the pinnacle of comfort and innovation within the Corvette Stingray lineup.
