The Chevrolet Corvette ZR1

The 2025 Chevrolet Corvette ZR1 is the latest addition to the Corvette lineup, delivering impressive performance through its new LT7 engine, a 5.5-liter twin-turbo V8 producing a stunning 1,064 horsepower and 828 lb-ft of torque. This engine, designed with a flat-plane crank similar to that of the Corvette Z06, makes the ZR1 the most powerful V8 ever manufactured by an American automaker. Capable of reaching a top speed of 233 MPH, it represents a major leap in Corvette performance.

The ZR1 comes with various track-focused options, like the ZTK Performance Package, which adds carbon-fiber elements for high downforce, including a prominent rear wing, front dive planes, and Michelin Pilot Sport Cup 2 R tires to boost grip. Chevrolet has also reintroduced the iconic split rear window, seen last in the 1963 Corvette, which now serves as a functional heat extractor for enhanced engine cooling.
